Legion of Bloom

The Legion Of Bloom

A bright, electric citrus cocktail with a sweet sugar rim and a refreshing lemon-lime finish.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz lemon-lime vodka
  • 1 oz blue curacao
  • Lemon-lime soda (to top)
  • Sugar (for rim)
  • Lemon twist

Instructions

  1. Rim a glass with lemon juice and dip into sugar.
  2. Fill glass with ice.
  3. Add vodka and blue curacao.
  4. Top with lemon-lime soda.
  5. Stir gently and garnish with lemon twist.
