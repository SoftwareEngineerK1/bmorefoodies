The Gronkarita

Vibe: Bold, loud, and full of championship flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Tequila Reposado
  • 1 oz Cranberry Juice
  • 1/2 oz Triple Sec
  • 1/2 oz Agave Nectar
  • Salt & Chili rim

Instructions

  1. Rim glass with salt and chili powder.
  2. Shake all ingredients with plenty of ice.
  3. Strain into the glass and serve.
