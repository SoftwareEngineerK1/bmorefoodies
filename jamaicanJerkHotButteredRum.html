Jamaican Jerk Hot Buttered Rum

Vibe: Spicy, savory-sweet, and warming.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Jamaican dark rum
  • 1/2 cup hot buttered batter (butter, sugars, 1 tbsp Jamaican jerk spice)
  • 4 oz boiling water

Instructions

  1. Combine hot buttered batter and rum in a mug.
  2. Stir in boiling water until smooth and steamy.
  3. Serve hot with an optional cinnamon stick garnish.
