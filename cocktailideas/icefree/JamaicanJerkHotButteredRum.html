Jamaican Jerk Hot Buttered Rum

A spicy, savory-sweet hot cocktail — rich buttered batter, Jamaican dark rum, and jerk heat.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Jamaican dark rum
  • 1/2 cup hot buttered batter (butter, brown sugar, white sugar, and 1 tbsp Jamaican jerk spice blend)
  • 4 oz boiling water

Preparation

  1. Combine hot buttered batter and rum in a mug.
  2. Stir in boiling water until smooth and steamy.
  3. Serve hot. (Optional: garnish with cinnamon stick or orange peel.)
