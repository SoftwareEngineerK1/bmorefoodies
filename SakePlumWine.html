Sake & Plum Wine (Umeshu)

Vibe: Simple, fruity, and balanced.

Ingredients

  • Junmai sake
  • Plum wine (Umeshu)
  • Optional: lemon peel or a small plum for garnish

Instructions

  1. Pour sake and plum wine into a glass (room temperature).
  2. Stir gently to combine.
  3. Taste and adjust the ratio to your preference.
