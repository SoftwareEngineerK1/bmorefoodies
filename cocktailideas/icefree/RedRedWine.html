Mulled Red Wine Royale

Vibe: Elevated, aromatic, classy.

Ingredients

  • Red wine
  • Orange slices
  • Cinnamon sticks
  • Star anise
  • 1 oz brandy (per serving)

Instructions

  1. Warm wine gently with spices (don’t boil).
  2. Add brandy to a mug.
  3. Pour spiced wine over it and serve warm.
