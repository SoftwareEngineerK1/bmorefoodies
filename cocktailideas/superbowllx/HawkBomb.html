Hawk Bomb

A quick, high-energy bomb shot—sweet melon and blue raspberry dropped into an energy drink.

Ingredients

  • 0.75 oz blue raspberry vodka
  • 0.75 oz melon liqueur
  • 4 oz energy drink

Instructions

  1. Pour vodka and melon liqueur into a shot glass.
  2. Fill a separate glass with energy drink.
  3. Drop the shot into the glass and serve immediately.
