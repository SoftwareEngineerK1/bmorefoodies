The Brady Bomb

A bold bomb shot—blueberry vodka and cranberry liqueur dropped into a chilled Red Bull.

Ingredients

  • 0.75 oz blueberry vodka
  • 0.75 oz cranberry liqueur
  • 4 oz Red Bull

Instructions

  1. Combine vodka and cranberry liqueur in a shot glass.
  2. Pour Red Bull into a rocks glass.
  3. Drop the shot into the glass and serve.
