Foxborough Fizz

A sparkling, red-white-and-blue inspired rum fizz with berries and a citrus twist.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz white rum
  • 1 oz blue curaçao
  • 0.5 oz grenadine
  • Club soda (to top)
  • Blueberries
  • Lemon twist

Instructions

  1. Fill a tall glass with ice.
  2. Add rum, blue curaçao, and grenadine.
  3. Top with club soda and stir gently.
  4. Garnish with blueberries and a lemon twist.
