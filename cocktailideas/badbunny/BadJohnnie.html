Bad Johnnie

Vibe: Tropical yet bold ‚Äî like island energy in a glass.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Johnnie Walker Black
  • 2 oz passion fruit juice
  • 3 oz coconut water
  • Fresh lime wedge (garnish)

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice.
  2. Stir gently, then strain into a chilled glass.
  3. Garnish with lime.
