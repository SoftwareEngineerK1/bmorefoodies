The Brady Bomb

Vibe: Precise, cool, and legendary.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz Blueberry Vodka
  • 1/2 oz Cranberry Liqueur
  • Top with Energy Drink
  • Fresh Blueberries

Instructions

  1. Add vodka and liqueur to a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake and strain into a tall shot glass.
  3. Drop into a half-glass of energy drink.
