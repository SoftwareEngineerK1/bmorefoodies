Emerald City Mule

A crisp, zesty mule with vodka, lime, ginger beer, and a touch of sweetness.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 4 oz ginger beer
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 0.5 oz simple syrup
  • Lime wedge

Instructions

  1. Fill a copper mug or tall glass with ice.
  2. Add vodka, lime juice, and simple syrup.
  3. Top with ginger beer.
  4. Stir lightly and garnish with lime wedge.
