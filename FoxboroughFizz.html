Foxborough Fizz

Vibe: A crisp, celebratory bubbly drink for the victory parade.

Ingredients

  • 1 oz White Rum
  • 1/2 oz Grenadine
  • Sparkling Wine (to top)
  • Maraschino Cherry garnish

Instructions

  1. Pour rum and grenadine into a flute.
  2. Top with chilled sparkling wine.
  3. Garnish with a cherry and enjoy.
