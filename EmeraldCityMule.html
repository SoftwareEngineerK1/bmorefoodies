Emerald City Mule

Vibe: Refreshing, sharp, and iconic.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Vodka
  • 1/2 oz Green Apple Pucker
  • 1/2 oz Lime Juice
  • Ginger Beer
  • Fresh Mint & Green Apple slices

Instructions

  1. Fill a copper mug with ice.
  2. Add vodka, pucker, and lime.
  3. Fill with ginger beer and garnish.
