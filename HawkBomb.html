Hawk Bomb

Vibe: High energy and tropical flavors for the 12th Man.

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Coconut Rum
  • 1/2 oz Blue Curacao
  • 2 oz Pineapple Juice
  • Splash of Lemon-Lime Soda

Instructions

  1. Stir rum and pineapple juice in a glass with ice.
  2. Slowly pour Blue Curacao over the back of a spoon.
  3. Top with soda.
