Legion of Bloom

Vibe: Electric citrus and neon greens to match Seattle's defense.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Tequila Blanco
  • 1 oz Midori Melon Liqueur
  • 1 oz Fresh Lime Juice
  • 1/2 oz Simple Syrup
  • Sugar rim & Lime wheel

Instructions

  1. Rim glass with lime and sugar.
  2. Shake all ingredients with ice.
  3. Strain into the prepared glass.
