Hennessy de Coco

Vibe: Sophisticated island spirit with Caribbean flair.

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Hennessy V.S.O.P.
  • 1 oz coconut water
  • 1/2 oz guava juice
  • 1/4 oz pineapple juice
  • 1/4 oz lemon juice
  • Dash of simple syrup
  • Pineapple leaves (garnish)

Instructions

  1. Shake all ingredients with ice.
  2. Strain into a chilled glass.
  3. Garnish with pineapple leaves.
