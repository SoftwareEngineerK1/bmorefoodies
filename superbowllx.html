Hennessy de Coco

Vibe: Sophisticated island spirit with Caribbean flair.

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Hennessy V.S.O.P.
  • 1 oz coconut water
  • 1/2 oz guava juice

Honey Apple Toddy

Vibe: Comforting, aromatic, easy favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon or dark rum
  • Hot apple cider
  • 1/2 oz honey
  • Cinnamon stick & Orange peel

Instructions

  1. Pour spirits and honey into a heat-safe mug.
  2. Top with hot apple cider.
  3. Stir gently and garnish.

Jerk Buttered Rum

Spicy, savory-sweet hot cocktail with jerk heat.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Jamaican dark rum
  • 1/2 cup hot buttered batter
  • 4 oz boiling water

Instructions

  1. Combine batter and rum in a mug.
  2. Stir in boiling water until smooth.
  3. Serve hot.
