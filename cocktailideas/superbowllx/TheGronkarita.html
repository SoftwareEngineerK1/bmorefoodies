The Gronkarita

A berry-powered margarita twist with a spicy rim and a splash of energy to keep the party going.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 1 oz triple sec
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 1 oz mixed berry puree
  • Splash of Red Bull
  • Spiced rim (salt + chili powder)
  • Fresh fruit garnish

Instructions

  1. Rim glass with lime and dip into spiced salt.
  2. Add tequila, triple sec, lime juice, and berry puree to a shaker with ice.
  3. Shake well and strain into rimmed glass with ice.
  4. Add a splash of Red Bull.
  5. Garnish with berries or lime.
